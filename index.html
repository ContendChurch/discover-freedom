Discovering Freedom

This is Freedom

We believe that Jesus didn’t just die to get us into Heaven; He died to get Heaven inside of us! We believe that there is a lifestyle that exists where we learn to live FROM Heaven TO Earth. His Kingdom and His peace are ever increasing. Our job as Christians is to crush Hell for a living by spreading the very Kingdom that we carry.

We can't stay idle. We can't afford to cover our light any longer. There's a life filled with hope, joy, and peace and it's rooted in LOVE. Is the life you're living worth the price He paid? It's time for us to become equipped to live the ABUNDANT life that Jesus came to give us. It's time to become a people that destroy Hell for a living!

It's time to discover freedom.

Live Wild & Free
