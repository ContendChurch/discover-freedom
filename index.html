Discovering Freedom

This is Freedom

We believe that Jesus didn’t just die to get us into Heaven; He died to get Heaven inside of us! We believe that there is a lifestyle that exists where we learn to live FROM Heaven TO Earth. His Kingdom and His peace are ever increasing. Our job as Christians is to spread the Kingdom of God that we carry.

We can't stay idle. We can't afford to cover our light any longer. There's a life filled with hope, joy, and peace and it's rooted in LOVE. Is the life you're living worth the price He paid? It's time for us to become equipped to live the ABUNDANT life that Jesus came to give us. It's time to become a people that destroy Hell for a living!

It's time to discover freedom.

Live Wild & Free

1. ENGAGE

Freedom is experienced and expressed best in community. We were never meant to live alone! Begin by joining our Facebook group and commit to traveling this journey in community.

2. EQUIP

We've designed a short and easy course to equip you to live the life Jesus paid for you to have. Check out our course schedule for details about specific dates and times. We’re going to launch you into freedom by leading you on a journey of discovering:

3. EXERCISE

In order to ignite your Faith with His Grace, we're going to take your Faith through a spiritual Bootcamp. You'll coordinate with other members of the group to meet up in locations around the Austin/Metro area and begin to TRY what you've been learning!

4. EXPLODE

With a new awareness of your identity and reality of His Kingdom, you'll see how EXPLOSIVE you are in Christ! You'll never want to go back!

SCHEDULE/DETAILS

Discovering Freedom is scheduled to launch in April of 2018.

How do I sign up?

Visit our Facebook group and join it today to receive updates along the way.

How will the course work?

Most of the course will work like an online Bible study. The community is built around a simple, closed Facebook group. The course is broken into 8 lessons and we'll be covering 1-2 lessons each week. Don't worry, the videos are only 5-10 minutes long! Each lesson will be accompanied by discussion in the Facebook group and the group will coordinate times and locations for meetups to EXERCISE your faith. Additionally, we'll have scheduled Q&A sessions via Facebook Live.

How much does it cost?

Absolutely nothing.

How can I help?

Help us spread the word and get more people involved! If you're interested in being an organizer for meetups in your area (or you have a space you'd like to donate), please email me.
